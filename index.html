About Homer Simpson

Who is Homer Simpson

Homer Simpson is a cartoon character. He is silly and naive, yet famous for being funny and emotional.

Homer Simpson at work.

Homer's Main Characteristics

  1. He has a family of 5 living in Springfield.
  2. He works at the nuclear power plant in Springfield.
  3. He loves donuts.

Homer's Passions

Homer has many interesting passions. Amongst others:

  • Spending time with his friends at Moe's Tavern bar.
  • TV. Like every American, he loves watching TV and truly believes in what the black box tells him.
